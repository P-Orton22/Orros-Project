THE ORROS CAMPAIGNS.

The Orros Campagins is a website that collects together background material, campaign scenarios, and rulesets for the tabletop RPG Pathfinder, all set within a homebrew fictional universe called 'The Orros Empire'. Here you can find information about the geography, history, and culture of the setting. As well as a list of important characters, inspiration for creating your own sessions within the setting, and stat blocks for the native wildlife and major npc's.

If you have any questions about any of the rules presented here, queries about the background information, or would like to find a playgroup to join; feel free to contact us using the link provided above.
